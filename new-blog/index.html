Bunker Crawler

One of my main projects is a 2D game called Bunker Crawlers, made using p5.js and i startet this project in 2023. It's an adventure game where you explore an underground bunker filled with puzzles, challenges, and hidden secrets.

In Bunker Crawlers, you go through dark and creepy places, discovering the mysteries of a world after a big disaster. The game has interesting characters, detailed levels, and fun combat that keeps you excited. Whether you like exploring, solving puzzles, or action, this game has something for you.

I'm always working to make the game better, adding new features, and improving how it plays. Keep an eye out for updates, sneak peeks, and behind-the-scenes looks at how it's made. Your support means a lot to me, and I can't wait to share more about Bunker Crawlers with you!
